About My Cake Shop

A cakery or cake shop is a retail business specializing in producing and/or selling cakes; they may also sell cupcakes, muffins, sponges, as well as other baked goods that fall under the title of a cake.
Cake shops may also sell equipment and supplies for home cake baking, especially for cake decorating, but not all do this.
Another common but not universal sideline is special orders such as wedding cakes and elaborate birthday cakes.
